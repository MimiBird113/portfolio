A Little Bit About Me

I am a fantasy writer and responsive designer & developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting caught up in the allure of a new book, I can be found traveling, experimenting with food and searching for new stories.

After having moved abroad to start my career in who-knows-what, I decided it was time to stop guessing and instead launch something more meaningful. The journey to this discovery was a long one, in which I've worn many hats. I've been a tour guide, a graphic designer, a blogger, a translator, a teacher, a staff writer, and a bookshop keeper.

All this uncertainty means I learned a lot of things. I'd love to show you some of what I can do.
