I noticed in university that my varied skills and interests didn't really add up to a potential job description. And I asked around to try to find out what the heck I'm supposed to do with so many things I know how to do and do well.

Turns out, no one really has a good answer. So, I made my own. It took a while to figure it out, because what I am is not a conventional job title.

Yes, you could say I'm a writer, or a designer, or an explorer - but my great passion is telling stories. It doesn't matter the medium: a new website, a brand strategy, a published book, a translated work, and anything in between.

My mission is to help people experience the world with greater depth.
